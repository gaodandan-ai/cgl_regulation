Cgl Regulation Explorer

An interactive regulatory-metabolic analysis platform for Corynebacterium glutamicum. It integrates transcriptional regulatory networks, Random Forest-based edge confidence scoring, genome-scale metabolic model mapping, and ecCGL1 enzyme-constrained annotations to support regulatory-metabolic hypothesis generation and candidate engineering target prioritization.

Get Started: Search a locus tag (e.g. cg0350, cg0444) or common name (sigH, whiB4) in the left sidebar to generate a regulatory network.

Data Attribution

This platform integrates public databases and models. Please cite the original resources:

  • TRNs: CoryneRegNet 7 / Extended regulatory network & sRNA-mediated regulation
  • Metabolic Models: iCW773 genome-scale model (and iCGB21FR support context)
  • Enzyme Constraints: ecCGL1 parameter annotations
  • Pathway maps: KEGG-compatible pathway mapping
  • Methods: RF edge confidence, reaction-pathway mapping, engineering target ranking

Current Limitations

  • ML Scores: Edge confidence metrics are prioritization markers, not biophysical probabilities.
  • Mapping-only: Metabolic impact analysis is based on static structural mapping, not dynamic Flux Balance Analysis (FBA) calculations.
  • Enzyme annotations: ecCGL1 parameters are annotated for pathway context; no active enzyme-constrained simulations are run in v0.1.
  • Validation: All engineering prioritizations are hypothesis-generating; experimental wet-lab validation is required before drawing biological conclusions.

Examples & Case Studies

Reproducible example workflows demonstrating biological questions and strain engineering target analyses.

Pathway View

Glutamate-associated Regulatory Analysis

Question: Which transcription factors may regulate glutamate-associated metabolic modules?

Focuses on highlighting regulatory networks surrounding glutamate metabolism, mapping TF control over glutamate biosynthesis and transport.

Workflow Steps:

  • Identify all genes and reactions mapped to glutamate metabolism.
  • Retrieve upstream transcription factors targeting these genes.
  • Evaluate regulatory confidence scores and ecCGL1 constraint coverage.
Expected Outputs: Upstream TFs, Regulated Genes/Reactions, ecCGL1 Coverage.
Limitations: Predicted relationships; does not simulate metabolic fluxes; requires validation.
Pathway View

TCA Cycle Upstream Regulator Discovery

Question: Which regulators may influence TCA cycle reactions?

Focuses on the tricarboxylic acid (TCA) cycle, discovering transcription factors that target structural genes in citrate, succinate, or malate reactions.

Workflow Steps:

  • Identify genes and reactions involved in the citric acid cycle (TCA cycle).
  • Retrieve all transcription factors regulating these structural genes.
  • Sort regulators by target gene count and average link confidence.
Expected Outputs: TCA Genes & Regulators, Confidence Averages, RF vs Heuristic comparisons.
Limitations: Predicted links; physiological significance varies; requires experimental validation.
Engineering Targets

Amino Acid Biosynthesis Engineering Target Ranking

Question: Which TFs are prioritized as candidate regulators for amino acid biosynthesis?

Ranks transcription factors based on their potential impact on amino acid biosynthesis pathways, prioritizing candidates regulating multiple reactions.

Workflow Steps:

  • Query candidates prioritized by global metabolic impact.
  • Filter candidates by regulation of amino acid pathways.
  • Check recommendation levels, kcat coverage, and regulatory mode.
Expected Outputs: Target prioritization tables, recommendation levels, reaction/pathway counts.
Limitations: Prioritization score represents impact potential; requires validation.

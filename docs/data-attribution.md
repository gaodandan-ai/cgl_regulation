# Data Attribution & Reference Directory

Cgl Regulation Explorer integrates multiple public resources and model-derived annotations. The platform does not claim ownership of the original databases or models. Users should cite the original resources when using derived results.

---

## 1. Regulatory Network Resources

### CoryneRegNet 7
The transcriptional regulatory network (TRN) structure and transcription factor annotations are derived from the CoryneRegNet repository.
* **Citation**: Parise, M.T.D. et al. *CoryneRegNet 7, the reference database and analysis platform for corynebacterial gene regulatory networks.* Scientific Data 7, 142 (2020). [https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-020-0481-0](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41597-020-0481-0)

### Extended C. glutamicum TRN & sRNA Network
Includes post-transcriptional sRNA-mediated regulatory predictions and annotations.
* **Citation**: Parise, M.T.D. et al. *The transcriptional regulatory network of Corynebacterium glutamicum: an update.* BMC Genomics 12, 608 (2011). [https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2164-12-608](https://doi.org/10.1186/1471-2164-12-608)

---

## 2. Genome-Scale Metabolic Model Resources

### iCW773 Model
Structural gene-protein-reaction (GPR) rules, reaction equations, and subsystems are mapped from the genome-scale metabolic reconstruction *iCW773*.
* **Citation**: Zhang, Y. et al. *A new genome-scale metabolic model of Corynebacterium glutamicum and its application.* Biotechnology for Biofuels 10, 169 (2017). [https://doi.org/10.1186/s13068-017-0856-3](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13068-017-0856-3)

### iCGB21FR Model
Structural mapping validation is cross-referenced with the *iCGB21FR* high-quality metabolic reconstruction.
* **Citation**: Feierabend, M. et al. *High-Quality Genome-Scale Reconstruction of Corynebacterium glutamicum ATCC 13032.* Frontiers in Microbiology 12, 750206 (2021). [https://doi.org/10.3389/fmicb.2021.750206](https://doi.org/10.3389/fmicb.2021.750206)

---

## 3. Enzyme-Constrained Model Resource

### ecCGL1 Model
Reaction-associated kcat values, molecular weight parameters, UniProt IDs, and parent reaction variant associations are extracted from the *ecCGL1* model repository.
* **Citation**: Niu, J. et al. *Construction and Analysis of an Enzyme-Constrained Metabolic Model of Corynebacterium glutamicum.* Biomolecules 12, 1499 (2022). [https://doi.org/10.3390/biom12101499](https://doi.org/10.3390/biom12101499)

---

## 4. Pathway and Annotation Resources

### KEGG Pathways
Pathway identifiers, pathway categories, and functional descriptions are cross-linked with Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes.
* **Citation**: Kanehisa, M. and Goto, S. *KEGG: Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes.* Nucleic Acids Research 28, 27–30 (2000). [https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/28.1.27](https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/28.1.27)
* **Update Citation**: Kanehisa, M. et al. *KEGG for taxonomy-based analysis of pathways and genomes.* Nucleic Acids Research 51, D587–D592 (2023). [https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkac963](https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkac963)
